❓ Help
What is this?

A gallery of 32 phase-stepped visualizers for drone subsystems. Each shows code executing one phase at a time.

I'm new to drones — where do I start?

The ⭐ Top 5 section. Open Motor Mixer first, then FC Stack. Everything else builds on those two.

Why separate galleries?

Maker = general sensors/motors/code. Drone = flight-specific (PID, IMU, failsafes, FPV, VTOL).

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally. No tracking.
